Client Overview

In the footwear industry, accurate foot measurement is crucial for ensuring a perfect fit, reducing returns, and improving customer satisfaction. Traditional measurement methods rely on manual techniques that are often imprecise and inconsistent. Our client, a company specializing in footwear solutions and e-commerce, needed an advanced, automated system capable of analyzing foot dimensions from images and correlating them with standard shoe sizes (USA, UK, etc.).

The primary objective was to enhance the online shoe shopping experience by minimizing the guesswork involved in selecting the right size. The system aimed to provide precise measurements for the waist, ball, and instep of the foot using AI-driven image processing techniques. Additionally, the client required a solution that could integrate seamlessly into existing E-commerce platforms, ensuring customers could access accurate sizing recommendations in real time.
