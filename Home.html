Who We Are

The Kashrote Youth Welfare and Development Organization

KYWDO

Was founded by local youth in the aftermath of the 2022 floods in Gilgit-Baltistan. Guided by our motto, “From Relief to Resilience, United for Development,” we began by providing essential relief items to flood-affected families and have since grown into a movement for long-term community empowerment.

Certified Organization

Recognized for helping people without distinction of area, tribe, caste, or religion

Internal Networking

Building strong networks with other organizations to help people quickly and without delay.

Frequently Asked Any Kind

Of Questions

How can I join KWDO?

You can register by filling out our membership/volunteer form available at our office or online (if digital registration is enabled). Once registered, you will be added to our database and invited to participate in ongoing and upcoming welfare activities, such as relief drives, awareness sessions, and development projects.

How can I donate to support your welfare projects?

You can support us financially by donating directly to our official bank account or through our fundraising campaigns. In addition, you can contribute by providing food supplies, clothes, books, or medical equipment for our relief efforts. Every contribution, no matter how small, makes a difference.

Are your services for everyone?

Absolutely. Our organisation works on the principle of equality and inclusiveness. All of our services—whether it is flood relief, education, health, or skill development—are provided without any discrimination of tribe, area, caste, or religious affiliation.

Do you collaborate with other organizations?

Yes we believe in collective effort for sustainable development. KWDO works in collaboration with local NGOs, government bodies, and international partners to expand the reach of our projects and deliver maximum benefits to communities.
