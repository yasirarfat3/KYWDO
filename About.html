The Kashrote Youth Welfare and Development Organization

KYWDO.

Was founded by local youth in the aftermath of the 2022 floods in Gilgit-Baltistan. Guided by our motto, “From Relief to Resilience, United for Development,” we began by providing essential relief items to flood-affected families and have since grown into a movement for long-term community empowerment
